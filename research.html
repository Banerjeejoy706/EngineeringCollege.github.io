Research Ethics

Professional and Research Ethics

Research integrity and responsible conduct are cornerstones of HIT's research activities. Building a trusting relationship between HIT researchers and the wider society is essential. Therefore, it is a fundamental requirement of all concerned within the research community of HIT, be they undergraduate, postgraduate or higher-level students and faculty. All involved in the research community at HIT must take this pledge. It is mandatory that students/faculty of HIT sign a declaration of commitment to uphold the highest standards of research integrity. Researchers, at any level, are expected to adhere to HIT's code of ethics.
